Pasta Flyer

I really like Pasta Flyer because it's never crowded and the food is always made fresh. There's plenty of room to sit, and there are a lot of options on the menu. It's good for sitting down to eat, or taking food to go between classes. They also offer a student discount, so it's relatively affordable. It's one of the few places where you can get cooked pasta and it's not over $15 - last week I went somewhere two blocks down the street and paid $25! They offer a lot of different customizable menu options to cater to every taste.

This dish has the most delicious sauce ever!
